### Think Again

Is Odisha only temples and beaches?

Or is it a living landscape of rivers, forests, forgotten villages,
rituals, stories, and places that quietly shaped centuries of culture?

Most journeys pass through Odisha.  
Very few truly explore it.

**Unfold Odisha** is an attempt to slow down and look again.

Here you will find districts, places, and cultural notes that may help you
see the land differently — not as a destination, but as a living narrative.
